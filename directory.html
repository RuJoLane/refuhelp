We can help you in several ways - see below for more information

ESOL

There are free English classes every Monday, Wednesday and Friday 6pm-8pm at Chester College. You can just turn up and enrol on the day you want to start.

ESOL at Chester College Monday, Wednesday, Friday 1800 - 2000

Health

We can help you register with a GP or a dentist at our health drop in service at Chester Community Centre every Monday 10am - 1pm.

For urgent help you can go to A&E at Chester Hospital, River Street, CH1 1BB 24 hours a day 7 days a week. It is free.

You can call 111 for all health problems. You can ask for a translator

Back to top of page

Legal Advice

There is a free legal advice service at Chester Community Centre every Wednesday 10am - 3pm. You can drop in, you don't need an appointment. We can help you find a solicitor and we can give basic advice on most immigration issues.

Chester Community Centre, Racecourse Road CH1 1AA

Wednesday 10.00 - 15.00

Back to top of page

Food and Clothes

If you need food call the National Food Bank Helpline on 0800 123456 and give your postcode. They can tell you where you nearest food bank is. They have translators.

If you need clothes you can visit the REFU-HELP charity shop. We give out free clothes every Thursday 1pm - 3pm.

Food help - call 0800 123456 - translators available

Free clothes - REFU-HELP Charity Shop, Chester Lane CH1 2AA. Thursday 1300-1500
